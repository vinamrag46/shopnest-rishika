How Escalation Works

Every message is checked for these signals before Rishika replies. If one matches, she doesn't try to solve it — she hands it off, with context, to the right team.
📩 Message received
→
🔍 Checked against escalation signals
→
🚦 Routed to team + urgency set
→
📝 Internal handover note created
SignalRouted ToUrgency
Fraud, hacked, unauthorized orderFraud TeamCritical
Refund dispute mentioning consumer courtSenior SupportHigh

Data leak or compromise concernData Privacy OfficerCritical
Threat of social media, press, or executive escalationPR & Executive SupportHigh
This check runs two ways at once: a simple keyword-based rule (independent of the AI) and the AI model's own instructed judgment. Running both means escalation doesn't depend solely on the model's behavior in any one moment — if the AI's wording ever drifts after a prompt change, the rule-based check still catches the same signals.
