Methodology
Each of the 100 queries was scored on four dimensions: whether it was resolved on the first message, whether escalation-worthy messages were routed to the correct team, how long the reply took to generate, and a 1–5 quality rating per language. First-contact resolution was intentionally low because the agent is instructed to always verify order/account details before resolving — a safety rule, not a defect. Escalation accuracy and reply quality are treated as the primary health signals for this system.
